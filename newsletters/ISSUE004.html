Welcome to the Chubbs Newsletter - Pizza Edition. As always, thanks for being  a part of Chubbs, it means the world to us to have you as a reader. This has been a very intense week for the team, as we had our #2015PizzaTour and several changes within the team. On to the news:
 
  • J&B is basically on hold, Jess seems to be more interested in our CTFO's technical skills than in other types of skills. She is in need of a technical expert because someone "hacked her company's website". Interestingly enough, systems security and cyber is one of Chubbs' core competencies (contact info@chubbs.solutions for a free quote). Jason is still struggling with pricing, because he's not sure whether to charge money or not to his future wife. I understand how this can be awkward.
 
  • We are sad to report that our CEO has been asked to step down. The board has lost trust in him and, after some discussion, it's been decided to transfer him to Prestige Worldwide as Talent Acquisition. This means that we are actively searching for a new chief executive. If you believe you have what it takes to lead a cutting edge, forward-looking startup and want to apply for the position, please contact our CFO (me) at bob@chubbs.solutions. We will be happy to consider and reject your application. This was our former CEO's reaction after being asked to step down.
 
 
  • We got new headshots for the upcoming website redesign, while the plan was to post the pictures here, our technology experts have advised us against this to avoid the newsletter getting stuck in the 'Spam' folder.
 
  • The #2015PizzaTour took place last Saturday. While we were expecting a luxurious limo crowded with hungry stomachs we had to settle with a Jeep with four hungry stomachs. This doesn't mean it was not a success, it was. We feel we are the most educated individuals when it comes to pizza places in Fort Wayne.
 


 Because of our CEO and Capital Adventurist bailing on us, we had to eat more slices in each place, making it hard for the team to reach our goal of visiting 8 places. This picture pretty much sums it all up:

 

We are now planning the #2015TacoTour and the #2016PizzaTour that should take place in Europe. As always, just contact us if you are interested in joining the team for this international event as tickets are limited.
